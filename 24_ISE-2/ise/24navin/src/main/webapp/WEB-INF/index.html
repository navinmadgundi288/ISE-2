OUR PROGRAMS

CIVIL ENGINEERING

The Department of Civil Engineering at Walchand institute of Technology Solapur was established in the year 1983. The following courses are being offered in the department -B. Tech in Civil Engineering (UG Course) with Honors Degree in Infrastructure Engineering or Honors Degree in Sustainability Engineering -Masters in Civil-Structural Engineering (P.G. Course)-Doctoral Program (Ph D). The Department is known for its reputed faculty having expertise in diverse fields.

COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

This department was established in 1985. Since then, the department has been running the UG course of CSE. The department started a PG course in Computer Science & Engineering in 2009-10. Research laboratory under the department is recognized for Doctoral Research. The department is equipped with the latest computer machines installed with the latest softwares. We have five computer laboratories which are equipped with the latest terminals, scanners, printers, CD/DVD…

ELECTRONICS AND COMPUTER ENGINEERING

The Electronics Engineering Department was established in 1983. Since then the department is running a U.G. course of Electronics Engineering. Department has started a PG course in Electronics Engineering from 2009-10. Research laboratory under the Department is recognized for Doctoral Research. Electronics Engineering Program is designed to systematically nurture students in six streams – Foundation of Electronics, Digital Electronics, Telecommunication…


ELECTRONICS AND TELECOMMUNICATION ENGG.

The Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Programme of Walchand Institute of Technology was established in the year 1999 with the intake of 40 seats. In 2002, the intake was increased to 60 seats while in 2010, the total intake of 120 seats was sanctioned. The program runs in two shifts since 2010.It has grown over the years into a competent wing with state of the art computing facilities, well equipped laboratories and dedicated faculty. The Programme is now recognized as a research.
